Bio

After graduating from the University of California, San Diego, I wanted to move somewhere with more trees. I googled "top dog friendly places to live" and found that Colorado was on all of the lists. And with that, I promptly packed up and moved here. I had never visited Colorado previously but I enjoy hiking, camping, snowboarding, and rock climbing so there was NO WAY that I would not like Colorado. Luckily I was right!

I currently live in the CapHill neighborhood of Denver with my two dogs Parker (10 year old Toy Aussie) and Grayham (8 yeard old herder mix).

Grayham and I have been a registered animal therapy team since 2013.
