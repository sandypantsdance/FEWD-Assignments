Bio

After graduating from the University of California, San Deigo, I wanted to move somewhere with more trees. Naturally, I googled "top dog friendly places to live" and found that Colorado was on all of the lists. I had never visited Colorado previously but I enjoy hiking, camping, snowboarding, and rock climbing so there was NO WAY that I would not like Colorado. Luckily I was right!

I live in the CapHill neighborhood of Denver with my two dogs Parker (10 year old Toy Aussie) and Grayham (8 yeard old herder mix.) Grayham and I have been a registered animal therapy team since 2013.

Experience

I have worked with animals in a variety of settings:

  • Boarding facility
  • Doggy day care
  • Veterinary hospital
  • Dog breeder
  • Animal shelter
  • Animal rescue
  • Research laboratory
  • Animal assisted therapy

During my time at the San Diego Humane Society and SPCA, I worked as an adoption counselor and learned behavior modification skills. I also had the privilege of working in the first 24 hour kitten nursery in the country where I cared for neonate kittens.
